Intention vs Expectation

Intention and expectation are two different animals. Intention is pure, free of the chains of outcome. Expectation drags you into the drama of life, making you a slave to circumstances. Intention is about expression, about channeling your energy into the world. Life is, at its core, an expression of energy.

Well-being

Chasing validation is a psychological trap. The need to be acknowledged and included stems from deep, primal urges. These urges create a dependency that controls us. Awareness is the antidote. By becoming aware of these processes, we achieve true well-being.

Beyond Chatter

Our minds are intricate, far too complex to be boxed into a self-image or ego. The secret to capturing the essence of life is simple: stay present. The ego limits you. Beyond the noise of the mind lies a profound experience.

Inner Exploration

We are our minds. Often, our minds run on autopilot, bouncing from thought to thought. Yet, simply observing these thoughts without force calms the mind. Exploring the depths of your own consciousness transforms your perception of life. Missing out on this inner exploration is a true loss.

Thoughts, feelings, emotions, beliefs change continuously. An appreciation of this fact is needed for a balanced life. Almost nothing of me remained from a decade ago. The contents of my consciousness and body have changed completely. But, there is an element in me that stitches a sense of continuity. The grip of the ephemeral loosens when one becomes aware.
